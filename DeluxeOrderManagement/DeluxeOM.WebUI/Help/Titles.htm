Titles

The Deluxe Avails application enables you to manage titles. The Title screen is used to compare the data from the Announcement file with the data available on Apple iTunes. This ensures that movies launch on time and prioritizing of orders is hassle-free. The Title report contains up-to-date information of all the titles on Apple iTunes, along with the status and delivery date, thus improving order creation. The Title screen allows you to do the following:

  • Store checks: Store checks are performed to ensure that movies launch on time. This is done by comparing announcement dates and assets (subtitles/dubs) with the titles available on Apple iTunes. This process of finding which titles are due within a time frame, helps in prioritizing escalations. Store checks also ensure that after the launch date of a movie, Deluxe looks into the status and prices of titles on the Apple iTunes store.

  • Region reporting: Region reporting ensures that we filter the data by region or territory. This allows Deluxe to talk to the WB group about the upcoming titles in their region.

  • Research on title assets: WB can use the language filter to plan a promotion or improve localization. This is done by requesting details on titles that have a particular language. WB can also use the territory filter to request details for a particular territory or region. This helps WB to determine whether they are underserving a particular market or not.

  • Backfill projects: The Backfill projects operation enables creation of orders (AOU/MOU) by filling the gaps identified during research on title assets. New assets are created using the existing announcement and data from Apple iTunes. These assets become available after a title is live in store.
