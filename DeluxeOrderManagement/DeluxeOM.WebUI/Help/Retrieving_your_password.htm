Retrieving your password

If you forget your password, Deluxe Avails application enables you to retrieve your password. You can retrieve your password with the help of your email ID.

 

To retrieve your password:

1.    On the Deluxe Avails log in page, click the Forgot your password? link.

The Forgot your password? page is displayed.

2.    On the Forgot your password? page, in the ENTER YOUR EMAIL box, type your email ID, and click EMAIL LINK.

A link for resetting your password is sent to your email ID.
